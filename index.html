Our Distribution & Network

At Aadgad Remedies, we understand the importance for people to receive medications and therapeutics instantly, in the time of need. Therefore, we go an extra mile to ensure continuous availability of medicaments to everyone. We have tied up with a huge network of manufacturers and healthcare providers within the country’s medical supply chain, to support and distribute pharmaceutical products ranging over treatment of varieties of ailments and illnesses like Anemia, Arthritis, Acute Muscular and Skeletal Pain, Hyperuricemia, Emesis, Acidity problems, and more.
